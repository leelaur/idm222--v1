Mythical Creature Wallet

Goal: Design a wallet that associates with the mythical creature, the gnome.

Research

What is a wallet?

  • The first wallet was used to carry coins but evolved to carry paper currency in 1980, and evolved again to carry cards in the 20th century
  • Most wallets were designed to fit in your pocket

What is a Gnome?

  • Gnomes protect treasure hidden underground where they live
  • Gnomes are known to be guardians of the earth

Observations

How do people use there wallets? What kind of wallets do people use?

  • People take swift motions to get out money and return to pocket
  • Most people used bi-fold wallets
  • Most people would just shove change into pocket

Storyboard

What does the user need?

  • An easy way to fit into pocket
  • A quick way to take out and put in money

Ideations and Iterations

Final Design

The length of the wallete represents the depth and layers of the ground where the gnomes live and when you put coins in this wallet it becomes the treasure that the gnomes protect underneath. I added a zipper to make the coins at the bottom more accessible. I also added card slots and an ID holder for different wallet items. I folded it in half where it can be used horizontally with the zipper or as originally designed. This also alludes to the gnomes small size.

Final Thoughts

his project has taught me a lot about the design process and made me realize how much thought goes into a single product. One thing I struggled with was creating 50 iterations because I ran out of ideas of ways to stretch a wallet as a gnome. However, I have enjoyed getting to use my creativity and pushing my thinking to build my gnome-wallet!
